# OpsCore

## Operations Management Platform

A role-based operations management platform built to centralize attendance, accountability, recruitment, finance, scheduling, reporting, and organizational workflows for a 160+ member student organization.

Built by a Management Information Systems student at Iowa State University with minors in Applied AI and Data Science, this project was created to replace disconnected spreadsheets, paper processes, group chats, and manual reporting systems with a single operational platform.

As Vice President of a large student organization, I wanted a system that could provide leaders with real-time visibility into organizational performance while reducing administrative workload and improving accountability.

---

## Project Overview

OpsCore is designed as a centralized command center for organizational operations.

The platform combines:

- Attendance Management
- Recruitment CRM
- Financial Tracking
- Meeting Notes & Documentation
- Calendar & Event Management
- Officer Accountability
- Reporting & Analytics
- Judicial Board Case Tracking
- Academic Performance Tracking
- Philanthropy & Service Reporting
- Role-Based Permissions

The goal was to create a system where officers could access the information they need without relying on multiple disconnected tools.

---

## Why I Built It

During my time serving as Vice President, I noticed that organizational data was spread across:

- Excel spreadsheets
- Google Drive folders
- Paper attendance records
- Group chats
- Shared documents
- Individual officer systems

This made reporting, accountability, and decision-making difficult.

OpsCore was developed to bring all of these workflows into one place while providing leadership teams with real-time operational insights.
